Conclusions About ☰

  - This graph shows that more than double the men have a higher chance of being under the risk of having a health issue related to alcohol compared to females.
- The graph shows that risks for men are increasing at a much faster pace compared to females.

  Drinking habits of males vs females were displayed on a bar graph through the use of the countif formula - This formula was used because there were questions which have a range of selections, in this case, how often people drink. This is a closed question which cannot be quantified therefore the countif formula was used.

 

 

  Another countif formula was used for qualitative data to gather a tally of all people who have gotten into different types of public incidents because of alcohol. - This formula was used because there were questions which have a range of selections, in this case, public incidents that people get into because of being drunk. This is a closed question which cannot be quantified therefore the countif formula was used.
