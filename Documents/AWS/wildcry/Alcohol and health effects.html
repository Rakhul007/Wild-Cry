Conclusions About ☰

 As can be seen in the graphs, Males have a much higher health risk than females, almost triple the amount during some years. The risks for both genders don’t seem to be increasing or decreasing throughout the years and are remaining at a steady pace. Therefore, this somewhat agrees to my hypothesis as, Males do have a much higher health risk although the risks which cause death don’t seem to be increasing.
